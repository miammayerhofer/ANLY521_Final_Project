About our Summarizer:

The 2 abstractive summarization models use the T5 Small Text-to-Text Transfer Transformer developed by Google with a HuggingFace summarization pipeline. The models are catered to anti-LGBTQ+ legislation.

Creators: Corrina Calanoc, Mia Mayerhofer, Madelyne Ventura, and Paul Wang

About this App:

This app was built with Flask framework, w3.css (modern css framework), and our custom abstractive summarization models. The link to our GitHub repository is located in the top right corner of the page.
